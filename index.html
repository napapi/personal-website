Stories of Sensei Napat

950AD

The Original Sensei Napat was born on a mountain in Khmer, a place in Southeast Asia. During 950, when he was just a little boy, he was sent to battle against the Champa empire. With no armor and a homemade sling, against ALL odds, he curageously charged at the battlefield.
